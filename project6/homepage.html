Our Nelson Park Branch is planning a bunch of fun activities for the whole community every week! We’re excited to expand our list in the coming weeks and make these programs and activities available to our Nelson Park community. Below is where you’ll find our recurring events and activities and a calendar for the library's schedule.

PROGRAMS

After-School Tutoring - Starting March 2024!

  • A new tutoring program in collaboration with our local Nelson Park High School that will provide English, math, science, and history help to kids from K-12.
  • Mondays and Tuesdays from 4:00 pm to 5:20 pm, Saturdays 1:30 pm to 4:15 pm
  • Located in the East Room on the first floor

ACTIVITIES

Crochet Workshop

  • A biweekly meeting hosted by our resident librarian Mr. Brothwell where you can learn to crochet or just hang out with and meet new people.
  • Wednesdays and Thursdays from 3:00 pm to 5:00 pm
  • Located in the General Lounge on the basement floor
